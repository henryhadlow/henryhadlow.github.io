About me

I’ve worked in agile, multi­disciplinary teams for almost all my working life – at GDS and previously at start-up seditionart​.com. I’m used to working with delivery managers, technical architects, developers and user researchers.

I know how to get software built. I know enough about application stacks, data­bases and frame­works to know what's quick to build and what takes longer. And I know how to divide up work in a way that makes sense to developers.

I've built platforms from scratch, and helped existing platforms to grow. I like to work with a user researcher to build a clear picture of who is using the product and why. This makes it easy to create a realistic strategy for growth.

Everything I build is beautifully designed. I worked for several years as an inter­action designer, and before that, a graphic designer. I’ve got an eye for an elegant user flow and a stylish typo­graphic detail.
